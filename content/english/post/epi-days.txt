### PHHP & EPI Research DAY


This year, I had the great honor of representing the Department of Biostatistics at the PHHP & EPI Research Day.
I presented my recent work in both oral and poster formats:

Mosquito Salivary Protein Antibodies as Efficient Endpoints for Vector Control Trials: A Mechanistic Comparison with Clinical and Entomological Outcomes

I am deeply grateful to my advisor [Dr. Matt Hitchings](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=YIehGAYAAAAJ&hl=en) and my co-authors [Dr. Libin Rong](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=s1yOtzMAAAAJ&hl=en) and [Dr. Derek Cummings]( https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=KnuWRoAAAAAJ&hl=en) for their invaluable guidance and collaboration throughout this work.
